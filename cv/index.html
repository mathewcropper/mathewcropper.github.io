I'm a Product Manager at Zendesk, building our vision for in-app relationships, which is used in thousands of apps all over the world. I'm interested in customer experience, communication technologies, and smart working.

Zendesk builds software for better customer relationships. We empower organizations to improve their customer engagement and better understand their customers. Zendesk was built upon a simple idea: make customer service software that’s easy to use and accessible to everyone.

I'm the Product Manager for Zendesk's Software Development Kits (SDKs). Our SDKs allow customers to bring self-service, ticketing, chat, and app rating features to their apps, seamlessly integrated with Zendesk Support and Zendesk Chat.

I work with a team of engineers in Dublin to build and enhance our SDKs, as well as working closely with other Product Managers, engineers, and marketing professionals all over the world.

We brought industry-leading self-service options to web and mobile, as well as a host of awesome non-player facing improvements in our support organisation.

Accenture is a leading global professional services company, providing a broad range of services and solutions in strategy, consulting, digital, technology and operations.
