One of the things on my mind a lot right now is ‘culture’. What kind of work environments do I thrive in? What’s important to me? What kind of values do people think are important to me, and do I live them? What values are important to those around me and the company I work for? Do I respect those values? Do I agree?

Lots of pondering, I’m sure you’ll see.

In my research I came across this great post on SlideShare, [Culture](https://www.slideshare.net/reed2001/culture-1798664 "Culture") from [Reed Hastings](http://www.slideshare.net/reed2001). It’s all about culture and values at [Netflix](https://www.netflix.com). It’s inspiring how simple they are, how much confidence is placed in people, and how these aren’t just buzzwords, but rather lived and meaningful values.
