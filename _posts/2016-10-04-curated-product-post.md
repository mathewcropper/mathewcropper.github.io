## <a name="design-principles"></a>GDS Design Principles

I'm positively evangelical about the stance that the UK Government Digital Service (GDS) takes to building products and services. Their design principles are something to live by, and should, IMHO, be part of every Product Manager's toolkit.

[Take a look at their design principles here.](https://www.gov.uk/design-principles) It starts with user needs, as it should.
