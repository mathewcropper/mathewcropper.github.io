---

We’re using [Trello](http://trello.com) for a project at the moment, and also plan to employ kanban style work in progress (WIP) limits to our various stages. There isn’t a way to do that natively in the application, though.

Enter the [Kanban WIP for Trello](http://gocrop.it/4) Chrome browser plugin. Once installed it’s really easy to use.

1. Enable the plugin
2. In the header of each column enter the WIP limit at the end of the title in square braces (eg. Dev [4])
3. The column now changes to yellow/amber when the WIP limit is reached, or red when it is exceeded

I like that the plugin doesn’t force the limits (ie. we have the flexibility to exceed a limit if we have a reason to do so), but does make it very clear visually what’s going on.
